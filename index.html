CICE

Creativity and Innovation cell in Electronics is an Electronics oriented society of JIIT Noida. Not only do we teach the students about several extreme useful components of electronics, PCB fabrication to develop microcontroller boards like Arduino and AVR but we also guide them to develop some unique ideas to outbid their knowledge toward forging some excellent projects in the future. We believe in excellence hence apart from workshops we also mentor the students in their self-learning and project making throughout the year.
